ISSUE #7 - 2021-03-07

Hello Friend!!

First week of March done! How was it for you? For me I attended GatsbyConf 2021, I started moving my blog over to Toast, got familiar wih Cloudinary and played with Vite!

GatsbyConf 2021

I joined a watch party of the conference with some friends when Gatsby v3 was announced with some real nice quality of life improvements for developers. If you follow me on Twitter you may have seen my yolo update to v3.

In addition to the YOLO update I used the codemod to switch from gatsby-image to gatsby-plugin-image no big deal on my site as there were two places that used it. I was quite seemless with only a couple of bumps with setting the correct placeholder for the images.

I spent some time yesterday removing my site's dependency Kwes forms, now that I'm using SendGrid for this newsletter there's no need for the additional script overhead.

Thanks to Paul Scanlon for taking a look a my site and coming up with the suggestions, now scottspence.com is a lot leaner! Thanks Paulie!!

Toast and Cloudinary

I've been playing around with Toast the last few days getting all my blog content in there to see how it preforms and currently I can build in just over 30 seconds!

In the process of getting set up with Toast I also started using Cloudinary and really like the out of the box optimisations you get with it!

I asked if anyone on Twitter uses Cloudinary to get an idea if the free tier usage would be enough for my blog. I tagged Wes Bos because I know he uses it for some of his courses and his own site.

I was concerned about the transformations but James Q Quick cleared that up for me.

Wes Bos did a thing in response to that! 🤯

Also, whilst looking into Cloudinary a found a great Learn with Jason where Jason and Dom detail some of the Cloudinary features.

Vite

Vite was announced a week or so ago now (I pronounce it like fight but with a v, how do you pronounce it?) I watched Sunil Pai on Learn with Jason where they showed off the build times with Vite and how to convert a Create React App project over to use Vite, impressive stuff!

It's bonkers fast, I tried it with a rect template and the dev server starts up in under half a second! ⚡
